Top Summer Camps in Switzerland

1. Alpine Bliss Camp 

Camp Alpine Bliss, draws both explorers and nature enthusiasts and is a great summer camp in Switzerland. It is situated in the gorgeous Swiss Alps and is surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush meadows. Whether you are looking for hiking, mountain biking, or outdoor survival training, everything is available here!  With the camp's eco-friendly approach to adventure, you'll have lifelong memories.

2. Camp Suisse

The popular international summer camp in Switzerland for kids and teens (7-17) is next on the list. The Camp Suisse is located in the magnificent Swiss Alps and provides a unique opportunity for adventure as well as cultural immersion. The activities at the camp are divided according to the age of the children, and everyone is guaranteed to have a great time!

3. TCS Camping Scuol in the Engadin Valley

An eco-adventure camp in the Engadin Valley is a camper's heaven and offers magnificent views of majestic peaks, lush mountain meadows. The camp is a perfect place for mountain lovers and offers fun activities like biking, camping and more.

4. Swiss Sports Performance Camp in Zurich

The Swiss Sports Summer Camp in Zurich will be enjoyable for athletes of all skill levels. This camp offers a variety of sports activities, including tennis, golf, and everything from football to horseback riding. With top-notch instruction and cutting-edge technology, you may enhance your abilities and take advantage of a summer filled with sports and fellowship.

Frequently Asked Questions
What camps are offered over the summer in Switzerland?

A wide range of summer camps are available in Switzerland, including sports camps, art and creative retreats, adventure camps, cultural immersion programs, and more. Every camper will find something because of the variety.

How long do Swiss summer camps typically last?

Each summer camp in Switzerland may vary in duration. While some camps only offer one week of programming, others offer for more. You can pick a camp based on your preferences and timetable.  

Is it safe to be part of summer camps in Switzerland?

Switzerland is renowned for being safe. The summer camps throughout the nation place a strong emphasis on children' health and safety.

What should I pack for a Swiss summer camp?

Depending on the Swiss camp and its activities, packing lists may change. The most crucial items to carry, however, are casual or hiking shoes, sunscreen, insect repellent, a reusable water bottle, and any other necessary personal goods.

Can I go to a camp in Switzerland without knowing Swiss, German, French, or Italian languages?

Although English is one of the official languages of Switzerland, several of its summer programs welcome participants from abroad. To guarantee a pleasant experience, it's a good idea to check the language requirements of your preferred camp.

Are parents or guardians permitted to accompany campers?

Each camp has its own set of visiting policies. While some camps can have more lax visitation policies, others would allow parents or guardians to visit on specific days. You must get special instructions from the camp administration.

How do I submit an application for a camp in Switzerland?

Swiss summer camps typically require applicants to complete an online registration form on the camp's website. You might be asked to share information about your hobbies, age, and specific requirements. The camp will assist you with registering after you submit your application.
