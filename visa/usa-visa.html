United States Visa for Indians

Pick your next Hassle-free holiday with us

A trip to the USA requires an American tourist visa for Indians. While visiting this land of endless opportunities and iconic landmarks, make sure you choose the right United States visa. From the Statue of Liberty to the Grand Canyon, the USA offers a unique blend of natural wonders and vibrant cityscapes. Obtaining a United States tourist visa can be a little daunting, but with right guidance it can be made easy. A USA visa ensures compliance with U.S. immigration laws and determines the purpose and duration of your stay.

Types of United States Visa For Travellers

Who Needs the US Visitor Visa?

The USA has a Visa Waiver Program (VWP) which exempts the citizens of a few countries like the UK, Australia, and Japan from any Visa to travel to the country. They can travel to the USA without a visa for up to 90 days. But, Indian passport holders must obtain a United States travel visa for any travel purpose. Indians must provide comprehensive documentation, attend an in-person interview, and meet specific eligibility criteria for their chosen visa type.

Tourist Visa USA Requirements

To apply for a USA tourist visa (B2), Indian applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • A valid passport with at least six months of validity beyond the intended stay.
  • A completed DS-160 visa application form.
  • A recent 2 x 2 inches sized photograph with a white background.
  • Proof of sufficient financial funds to cover the trip.
  • Travel itinerary, including flight tickets and accommodation details.
  • A cover letter stating the purpose of the visit.
  • Additional documents, such as proof of employment, tax returns, or property ownership, to establish ties to the home country.

The B2 visa is typically valid for a duration of up to 6 months per visit, with a validity period of 1 to 10 years, depending on the visa type and applicantâ€™s profile.

USA Tourist Visa Application Process Offline Process

Step 1: Complete the DS-160 form online and print the confirmation page.
Step 2: Pay the visa application fee at an authorized bank.
Step 3: Schedule an appointment for a visa interview in the U.S. Embassy or Consulate India.
Step 4: Attend the interview with all required documents.
Step 5: Await the visa decision, which is typically communicated immediately after the interview.

USA Visa Fees and Processing Time

The standard application fee for most non-immigrant visas, including the B1/B2 visa, is approx. INR 15,000. With additional fees, the visa fees may cost around INR 19,500. Processing times vary but currently range from 300-320 working days after the interview. For expedited processing, applicants can request premium services at an additional cost, subject to eligibility.

Latest Updates on USA Visa

Recent updates include extended visa interview waivers for certain renewal applicants. The US Embassy has also introduced measures to reduce wait times for appointments, particularly for B1/B2 visa categories. Beginning January 1, 2025, nonimmigrant visa applicants in the US will be permitted to change their interview appointment one time without facing any extra charges. If an applicant fails to attend the appointment or has to reschedule again, they must schedule a new appointment and pay the application fee again.

Tips for a Successful USA Visa Application

Securing an America tourist visa can be a little intimidating with so many documents and the whole interview process. But, with the right guidance and tips, you can get a successful visa for the USA. Here are some tips for a successful USA visa application:

  1. Fill out the DS-160 form accurately and without any mistake.
  2. Be well-prepared for the visa interview with all the necessary documents.
  3. Maintain a transparent and genuine purpose for your visit. Donâ€™t hide anything.
  4. Dress formally and communicate confidently during the interview.
  5. A well written Cover Letter makes a huge impact on the visa application. Hence, make sure a promising cover letter is written.

The rejection rate for US Visitor Visa is approximately 20% globally for B1/B2 visas. Make sure you don't make the following mistakes which might lead to visa rejection:

  • Insufficient financial fund proof.
  • Not showing proof of relevant ties to the home country.
  • Incomplete or incorrect application forms.
  • Suspicious travel intent or inadequate explanation.

Frequently Asked Questions
How long can I stay in the USA with a B2 visa?

You can stay in the USA for up to 6 months per visit.

Can Indians apply for a U.S. visa online?

Yes, the DS-160 form can be completed online, but an in-person interview is mandatory.

What documents are mandatory for a U.S. tourist visa?

A valid passport, DS-160 form, financial proof, travel itinerary, and more.

How early should I apply for a U.S. visa?

You must apply at least 2-3 months before your planned travel date.

Can I reschedule my interview appointment for a US visa?

Yes, you can reschedule your US visa application only once. In case you miss your rescheduled appointment, you will have to make a new, paid appointment.
