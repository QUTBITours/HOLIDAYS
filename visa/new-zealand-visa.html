New Zealand Tourist Visa for Indians

Take a trip to New Zealand and enjoy the breathtaking scenic landscapes, the unique Maori culture and the numerous activities to engage in to brighten up the trip. This comprehensive guide provides all the essential information about obtaining a New Zealand visa. Whether you're drawn to its pristine beaches, eager to trek through its majestic mountains, or keen to immerse yourself in its vibrant culture, securing a visa is a crucial step for your visit. Read on to learn everything you need to know about the New Zealand visa process.

Types of New Zealand Travel Visa

  • Tourist/Visitor visa: This visa is obtained by those who are visiting New Zealand for the purpose of tourism, leisure or sightseeing. 
  • Business visa: This visa is used by those who are visiting the country for any business-related purpose, such as attending meetings, conferences, events, investments, etc.
  • Transit visa: This visa is acquired by those who have a layover or transit in New Zealand while travelling to another country.

Documents Required for New Zealand Visitor Visa

Before going ahead with the application process for a visa to New Zealand, here are some of the documents that you need to be prepared with:

  • An accurately completed visa application form.
  • A valid passport with at least six months of validity and two blank pages left in the passport.
  • Proof of roundtrip flight bookings, bank statements showing sufficient funds to support the trip, accommodation and health insurance.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Cover letter explaining purpose of the visit.

How to Apply for a New Zealand Tourist Visa Online

  • Step 1: Visit the official New Zealand Immigration website.
  • Step 2: Register and create an account.
  • Step 3: Log in and select the type of visa to apply for. Fill in the visa application form accurately and completely.
  • Step 4: Upload scanned copies of all the required documents.
  • Step 5: Pay the visa processing fee and submit the application form.
  • Step 6: If required to submit biometric information, schedule an appointment at the nearest Visa Application Centre and provide biometric information on the appointed date.
  • Step 7: Track the status of the application using the tracking number and wait for the visa to get processed.
  • Step 8: Once approved, you will receive the visa via e-mail.

How to Apply for a New Zealand Tourist Visa Offline

  • Step 1: Visit the New Zealand Visa Application Centre website and download the application form or visit the nearest centre and collect the application form.
  • Step 2: Manually fill out the application form accurately and completely. 
  • Step 3: Gather copies and original documents that have been mentioned in the sections above.
  • Step 4: Visit the nearest New Zealand Visa Application Centre along with the visa application form and necessary documents.
  • Step 5: Upon arrival at the centre, submit the documents and the completed application form. Provide biometric information if necessary.
  • Step 6: Pay the visa processing fee at the centre and collect the acknowledgement receipt with the tracking number for the application.
  • Step 7: Using the tracking number, monitor the status of the application.
  • Step 8: Once approved, collect the passport with the visa stamp from the centre or have it couriered if already agreed upon.

Frequently Asked Questions
How long can I stay in New Zealand with a tourist visa?

Up to 9 months within an 18-month period.

Is travel insurance mandatory?

Yes, it is required for the visa application process.

Can I work on a tourist visa?

No, a separate work visa is needed for employment.

How early should I apply for my visa?

At least 2–3 months before your travel date.

Are biometric details mandatory?

Yes, for most visa categories.

What should I do if my visa application is rejected?

Review the reasons for rejection and reapply after addressing them.

Can I convert my tourist visa to a work visa?

No, you need to apply for a work visa separately.

Are there age restrictions for a work visa?

Yes, some categories may have specific age limits.
