United Kingdom Visa for Indian Tourists

Pick your next Hassle-free holiday with us

To plan a trip to the UK, the first step for Indian passport holders is acquiring a UK visa. The United Kingdom is a wonderful country to visit, a place filled with magic, charm and beauty like no other. You get to visit the iconic and popular landmarks and also immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of this incredible country and everything it stands for. However, Indians travelling to the UK require a visa, whether it is for tourism, education, leisure, business or visiting friends and family. Additionally, there is no visa on arrival for Indians travelling to the country, so ensure that you have obtained a visa prior to your trip.

UK Visa Types

Travellers That Require a UK Visa

All Indian passport holders travelling to the United Kingdom require a UK visa. This needs to be acquired prior to one’s trip. Please note that there is no option of visa on arrival for Indians, so plan ahead and get your visa secured ahead of your trip.

UK Tourist Visa Requirements and Validity

Before you go ahead with your UK visa application, there are certain things to keep in mind and prepare for. So, all Indians travelling to the UK who need to get a visa, make sure to follow these guidelines:

  • A valid passport that has a validity for at least 6 months.
  • Proof of funds to help finance the entire trip.
  • Proof of accommodation bookings or invitation from the host/owner. 
  • A detailed itinerary that includes flights, accommodation and activities.
  • Proof of return details via documents that signify employment letters or property deeds to assure return after the visit.

Validity:

  • Short-term Standard Visitor Visa: up to 6 months
  • Long-Term Standard Visitor Visa: up to 2 years, 5 years and 10 years.
  • Visitor in Transit Visa: until the transit goes through

UK Visa Application Process

The process of applying for a UK tourist visa may often be a daunting process. Here is a step-by-step guide to help all Indian travellers with the application process for a UK visa for Indian citizens.

  1. Step 1: Visit the official UK government website for Indians and fill out your online application form.
  2. Step 2: After filling out the application form, make sure to pay the processing fee.
  3. Step 3: Once you have received the confirmation, schedule an appointment at the VFS Global Application Centre for submitting your biometric data.
  4. Step 4:  While going for the appointment, make sure you take all required documents, including the printed application form, passport, photographs, and supporting documents.
  5. Step 5: After the biometric data is given, the application processing time usually takes about 3 weeks. 
    Step 6: Once your application is processed, collect your passport and the visa via courier or from the application centre.

United Kingdom Visa from India: Fees and Processing Time

The processing time for a short-term standard visitor visa, long-term visitor visa and transit visa is 3 weeks and it costs between Rs. 12,000-13,000 and Rs. 40,000-1,00,000, depending on the number of years. The validity for a short-term visa is 6 months and for a long-term visa, it varies from 2 years to 5 years to 10 years. 

Latest News and Updates about UK Tourist Visa

From 2025, the United Kingdom is introducing an e-visa for travellers from visa-exempt countries for this destination. But this does not apply for Indian citizens, as they require a visa to travel to the country. 

Tips For A Successful United Kingdom Tourist Visa Application From India

The UK visa has a high rejection rate. It is crucial that one follows all the steps meticulously to avoid rejections. Here are a few tips from our side to help ensure a successful visa process:

  1. Ensure all information is accurate and documents are complete.
  2. Provide clear evidence of sufficient funds.
  3. Demonstrate compelling reasons to return to India.
  4. Apply well in advance of intended travel dates to accommodate processing times.

Common Reasons for Rejection of UK Visa

Generally, the rejection rate for getting a UK visa is pretty high, with 15% of the visas being rejected. Here are some of the common reasons for visa rejection for the United Kingdom that Indian passport holders make and can be avoided:

  1. Lack of proof of adequate funds.
  2. Missing or incorrect documents.
  3. Ambiguity or unclear information regarding the purpose of the visit.

Frequently Asked Questions
How much is the UK visa fee now?

The UK visa fee varies based on the visa type. For Indian citizens, a Standard Visitor Visa for up to 6 months costs ₹12,933, while longer-term visas range from ₹48,584 (2 years) to ₹1,08,302 (10 years).

How much bank balance is required for a UK visa?

There is no fixed amount, but applicants must show sufficient funds to cover travel, accommodation, and living expenses. Typically, having a balance that covers Rs. 9,000-14,000 per day of stay is recommended.

Why is a UK visa so expensive?

UK visa fees cover administrative costs, security checks, and processing services. The fees also help maintain the efficiency of the UK immigration system and ensure timely processing of applications.

Is a UK visa easy to get?

A UK visa is relatively straightforward to obtain if applicants meet all requirements, provide accurate documents, and demonstrate strong ties to their home country. Thorough preparation improves the chances of approval.
