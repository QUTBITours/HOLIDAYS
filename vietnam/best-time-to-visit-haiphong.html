Best Time To Visit Hai Phong

Pick your next Hassle-free holiday with us

Vietnam tourism offers diverse experiences for tourists ranging from vibrant cities to scenic landscapes. Among the several hidden gems in Vietnam, Hai Phong, a coastal city in northern Vietnam is a must visit spot. Visiting Hai Phong during the perfect time while embarking on Vietnam tour packages will ensure you get the most enchanting experience.

When is it ideal or the best time to visit Hai Phong? Let's explore!

Best time to visit Hai Phong for a Breathtaking experience

Hai Phong experiences four distinct seasons which are to be taken into consideration when planning your Vietnam tour packages from India. The ideal time to visit this picture perfect city is between the months of September and October. During this period, you can experience mild and pleasant weather. The temperature ranges from 25 degree celcius to 30 degree celsius. With clear skies and without any heat or rain you can enjoy the breathtaking beauty of Hai Phong. Another best time for travelers to pay Hai Phong a visit is between the months of March and April. 

Seasons in Hai Phong

Spring

Spring in Hai Phong starts from February and goes on till April. The weather is very cool and pleasant during spring, making it an excellent time for tourists to enjoy exploring the city. This season marks the Tet Festival in Vietnam. The city comes to life with beautiful and bright decorations and traditional festivities. Witnessing this festival is definitely among the top things to do in Vietnam. During Spring you can also spend time by visiting the ancient temples and pagodas, thereby praying for prosperity and good fortune.

Summer

Summer in Hai Phong starts from May and goes till August. Summer in Hai Phong is the hottest season with high humidity. During Summer the temperature reaches up to 35 degree celsius. Irrespective of the heat, you can spend a great time exploring Cat Ba Island which is near Hai Phong and surely among the best places to visit in Vietnam. Here you can indulge yourself in water sports like kayaking and swimming, thereby making your Vietnam trip an adventurous experience.

Autumn

Hai Phong experiences Autumn from September to November. Autumn is the best time to visit Hai Phong, Vietnam. With clear skies and pleasant climate, Autumn season in Hai Phong is excellent to indulge in adventure activities like trekking in the nearby limestone karsts and enjoying the beautiful surroundings.

Winter

December marks the start of Winter in Hai Phong. Winter lasts till January. Hai Phong is relatively cold during this period. Temperatures drop to 10 degree celsius. Since it is not the most popular time for tourists to pay a visit, it is a unique opportunity for those travelers seeking a quieter atmosphere to experience the serene environment of Hai Phong.

Peak Season Vs Off Season

Peak Season

During the autumn months, Hai Phong experiences the surge in tourists. The weather is just perfect for exploring the top places to visit in Hai Phong including the iconic Du Hang Pagoda and the vibrant Cat Ba Island. The autumn breeze adds to the romantic environment, making Hai Phong one among the romantic places to visit in Vietnam for couples and honeymooners embarking on a Vietnam trip. You also get the opportunity to taste some delicious seafood dishes at the local markets and restaurants.

Off Season

Hai Phong experiences Off Season from May to August. Number of tourists visiting will be on a lower scale but it is an ideal time for tourists looking to enjoy the city's beautiful coastline. The nearby beaches offer a perfect opportunity for sunbathing and water activities. If you are okay with visiting during summer then you will find less crowds and more affordable accommodations in Hai Phong during this season.

Conclusion

Regardless of when you choose to visit Hai Phong, the city will enchant you with its unique and diverse beauty. Hai Phong offers an unforgettable experience throughout the year.
