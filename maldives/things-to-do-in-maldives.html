Things to Do in Maldives

Pick your next Hassle-free holiday with us

There are many things to do in Maldives that can be considered to make for a great time here. This destination is ideal for adventure and beach lovers as it offers an exciting experience, but it's also the best place for those who want to just lay back and relax by the shores of the crystal-clear waters and white sand beaches. The luxurious resorts provide tranquillity and comfort for the visitors and luxury treatments such as spas and delicious delicacies make the stay much more incredible. You can partake in thrilling water sports amidst stunning landscapes and natural beauty that provides for a wonderful experience. Additionally, the Maldives is a haven for nature lovers, as its absolutely breathtaking landscapes, colourful coral reefs, and diverse marine life attract the attention of tourists. But if all these are not enough, you will also experience the native cultural heritage and become one among the locals when you're in the more urbanised sections of the island. All in all, the Maldives is simply a complete package where you could enjoy the warmth of hospitality and rich culture as well as breathtaking stunning beauty, numerous activities and savouring mouth-watering local cuisine. The following are the exciting activities in the Maldives to make an unforgettable memory:

1.

Cultural Experiences

  • Visit Male City: This very lively capital city of Maldives will give you an enriching cultural experience when you are around. Visit iconic landmarks such as the Maldives National Museum, which gives a glimpse into the past as it showcases the ancient royal artifacts and Islamic treasures. For an additional experience, you can also visit the Grand Friday Mosque for its marvelous architecture or walk through the local streets and learn the way of life of the locals.
  • Experience the local island life: There are some islands in the Maldives that allow you to immerse yourself in the local culture and experience the life of the locals by going fishing, indulging in the native cuisine or even interacting with the warm and welcoming communities in islands such as Maafushi and Dhigurah.
2.

Romantic Experiences

  • Overwater dinner: If you want a private, unique, and romantic experience, then consider having a candlelight dinner on the deck of an overwater villa. There is no better way to spend your evening than being surrounded by tranquil waters, indulging in delicious food, and having the company of your partner, is there?
  • Sunset dolphin cruise: You could take a fun cruise offered by the many luxury stays and sail into the horizon. During the cruise, you get to experience the spectacular sunset paired with the playful leap of the dolphins, which gives the most wholesome experience to share with your partner.
  • Couple's spa: A honeymoon trip is incomplete if one doesn't get pampered at the luxurious spa treatments offered in the Maldives. From massage therapies to aromatherapy and much morerejuvenate together and make the most of your relaxing stay.
3.

Adventure Experiences

  • Scuba diving: If you are a person who loves to immerse yourself and be a part of the vibrant marine life, then there is no better place to go scuba diving than the Maldives. Having places such as the Banana Reef and Manta Point offers a magical experience for its divers as they become one within the sea and one among the marine life. Swimming alongside the vibrant coral gardens, manta rays, sea turtles, and even whale sharks would provide for quite a thrilling experience. And bonus points if you are a certified scuba diver, as you get to explore deep shipwrecks and underwater caves as well.
  • Snorkelling with whale sharks: If you have the opportunity to visit the South Ari Atoll, do not miss snorkelling with whale sharks. You get to see the whale sharks even if you are a beginner in snorkelling. Maldives is one of those places where the experience of snorkelling with whale sharks is uniquely thrilling and is available throughout the year; hence, there is no time limit for this once-in-a-lifetime experience.
  • Other water sports: If scuba diving or snorkelling is not your cup of tea, then do not worry. The Maldives caters to other forms of adventure activities for the adrenaline rush seekers. You get to engage in activities like jet skiing, windsurfing, surfing, wakeboarding, kitesurfing and even flyboarding. For a blend of beauty and adventure, try parasailing or paddleboarding during sunrise and take in the spectacular views of the island.
