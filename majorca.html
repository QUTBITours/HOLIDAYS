Majorca Travel Guide and Tourism

Pick your next Hassle-free holiday with us

Majorca also known as the Mallorca is the Balearic's largest island. The city is famous for its scenic landscapes, sunny beaches, astonishing mountains, expressive hill towns, breathtaking views, and delicious Mediterranean food. Consider exploring Spain tour packages to experience the diverse beauty and culinary delights of this enchanting destination.

Majorca is one of the favorite tourist destinations. It offers a lot of options for the tourists to explore. Alcudia's Old Town, Puerto Portals: A Glamorous Seaside Resort, The Hilltop Town and Monastery of Valldemossa, Llucmajor Beaches, The Port Town of Pollen√ßa, Cala Figuera and the Calvia Coastline are some of the main attractions of the city. Do not forget to visit the beautiful capital city Palma De Mallorca where you can relax along the seaside and also enjoy the culture of the city.

Getting in majorca

Palma de Mallorca Airport is the main international airport in Majorca. It is Spain's third largest airport and is 8 km away from the center of the city. It operates flights from major Indian cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Bangalore, Kochi, Hyderabad, Kolkata and Trichy.

Getting out of majorca

Palma de Mallorca Airport operates flights to many nearby countries like Switzerland, Belgium, France, Portugal and Italy. You can also visit these countries on your trip to Majorca.

Public Transport in majorca

The bus is the best way to explore the city of Majorca. The bus routes are extensive and they are also cheap. There are also express bus services available for certain destinations.
 

Taxi & Airport transfer in majorca

The taxi service is not quite popular in Majorca. There are bus services that operate from the airport to the city.

Transport & city passes in majorca

The carnet card is the travel pass for using the public transport. You should select your destination and also what kind of ticket you want for your stay in Majorca. You should opt for the non-resident carnet pass for your travel in public transport.

Driving & Car rental in majorca

You can rent a car from any tourist towns in Majorca. But during season time, make pre-booking because rental cars will be on demand.
