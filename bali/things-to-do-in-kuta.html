Things to Do in Kuta

Pick your next Hassle-free holiday with us

Kuta is a bustling beachfront town located in the southern part of Bali, Indonesia. It is known for its long sandy beach, vibrant nightlife, and an array of dining options. Kuta is popular among tourists for its surf-friendly waves and affordable accommodations. You will have so many interesting things to do in Kuta You can enjoy a variety of water sports, take surf lessons, or simply relax on the beach. Kuta is also a great base for exploring other parts of Bali and is worth adding to your Bali tour package. Bali trip packages.

Discover the best things to do in Kuta, Bali

Whether you're looking for a relaxing vacation or an adventurous one, Kuta has plenty of options for everyone to explore. Here is the list of things to do around Kuta, Bali:

2. Kuta Beach: A Tropical Paradise

1.

Enjoy your Day at Kuta Beach

One of the best things to do in Kuta is to spend a good time on Kuta Beach and soak in its sunset scene. You may spend all year-long tanning, surfing, swimming, and playing beach soccer at this location on the southwest coast of the island. Jalan Kartika Plaza and Jalan Pantai Kuta, Kuta's main beach roads, are lined with hotels, resorts, entertainment venues, retail stores, and a variety of restaurants and bars. The vibrant Kuta Art Market and Discovery Shopping Mall both offer shopping opportunities. The waves at Kuta Beach in Bali are appropriate for both novice and experienced surfers.

2.

Go on a Thrilling Adventure at Waterbom Bali

Within its 38,000 square meters of tropical gardens and swimming pools, Waterbom Bali provides exhilarating waterslides and family-friendly water games. In front of Discovery Shopping Mall, along Jalan Kartika Plaza, is where you'll find the waterpark. The Climax's super-loop body slide, the Superbowl, Boomerang, Smashdown, and Race Track are just a few of the thrilling attractions at Waterbom Bali, one of Bali's largest waterparks. You may eat Indonesian, Japanese, and Mexican cuisine at the on-site eateries of Waterbom Bali after working up an appetite. It is one of the best things to do in Kuta, Bali.

3.

Enjoy the sunset with cocktails at the Finns Beach Club

One of the best Bali things to do in Kuta is to experience a magical sunset while sipping on delicious cocktails at Finns Beach Club. This trendy hotspot draws people from all over the world, making it a great place to relax, meet new people, and enjoy stunning ocean views. The atmosphere is lively, and their extensive cocktail menu is sure to have something for everyone's taste. Treat yourself to a Bali getaway with this unforgettable experience.

4.

Have fun at the Circus Workshop at South Kuta, Bali

For a family-friendly experience and a break from typical Bali things to do in Kuta, try the Circus Workshop at South Kuta. This entertaining attraction is perfect for adults and children alike. Experienced trainers teach you various circus techniques, including juggling, acrobatics, and trapeze skills. It's not only a fun way to spend an afternoon but also an opportunity to learn something new and make lasting memories.

5.

Watch Plays in Kuta Theatre with Great Gusto

The Kuta Theater features weekly stage shows, with the majority of the performers dressed in spectacular Balinese dance costumes, and is one of the must-try stuff to do in Kuta. The program "Lights of Faith” is about an old-fashioned relationship dealing with the difficulties of modernity. There are exciting illusion scenes interspersed with lighthearted and humorous ones. The presentation also features magic acts, such as the traditional sawing of a woman in half, as well as plenty of distraction and disappearance effects, levitations, and displacements.
 

6.

Beat the waves at Armada Flow House

If you're looking for exciting things to do near Kuta that involve water sports, then look no further than Armada Flow House. This unique attraction offers endless fun as you try your hand at flowboarding on an artificial wave machine. Professional instructors are there to guide you while ensuring your safety, so you can enjoy this adrenaline-pumping activity. Release your inner surfer and challenge yourself with this thrilling experience.

7.

Catch the view of the Abandoned Plane

For those seeking offbeat things to do in Kuta Indonesia, visiting the mysterious abandoned plane is sure to pique your interest. Located on private property surrounded by trees near Pandawa Beach, this Boeing 737 is now a local tourist attraction that adds an air of intrigue to your trip. Although getting up close may be difficult, spotting it from various viewpoints should be achievable and worth the effort for those seeking something different.

8.

Delight your Kids in Circus Waterpark

Near the Ngurah Rai International Airport in Tuban, on Jalan Kediri, is a recreational area called Circus Waterpark. The waterpark offers numerous slides, a lazy river, and a separate kids' area with water cannons, fountains, and miniature train rides. The "speed slider" and "wave slides" are among the more thrilling slides that thrill-seekers can choose from. which makes it the best thing to do in Kuta. Additionally, the Circus Waterpark offers eateries, a foot reflexology massage, photography services, and temporary tattoos. Lockers, towels, and gazebos can all be rented for a fee if you want a private, shaded area to unwind. 

9.

Enjoy Cycling around Kuta on a Mobile Bar

Have you ever heard of "legal drinking and driving"? One of the most fun things to do around Kuta is to get on the Bali Beer Cart and go exploring like never before. You can select a 10- or 13-seater cart to go around some of the most picturesque areas of the city while being served ice-cold beer (choices of champagne and wine are also available at various price points). When visiting Kuta, you should do this because it offers a unique experience and a thrilling ride (literally).

10.

Don’t Ever Miss the 5GX Bali Reverse Bungy

In Legian, there is a thrilling "reverse bungee" experience called 5GX Bali Reverse Bungy. You just need to get in and buckle up to feel the unfathomable exhilaration of being propelled into the air at a mind-boggling speed of 200 km/h. According to their motto, 5GX provides the sole legal means of "getting high in Bali." Up to three people can experience this exhilarating ride at once, and the cost of the ride is dependent on the number of participants. Add this activity to your list of things to do in Kuta Bali.

11.

Go Shopping and Dining at Kuta Beachwalk

Kuta Beachwalk is the perfect destination for those looking for the best activities to do in Kuta that involve shopping, dining, and entertainment. With a wide range of boutiques and stores, including local handicrafts, branded items, and souvenirs, it's a must-visit spot for shopaholics. Additionally, the complex boasts numerous dining options featuring both local and international cuisine to satisfy your cravings after a long day of exploring all that Kuta has to offer.

12.

Savour the native delicacies at the Warungs in Kuta

One of the best things to do in Kuta is to treat your taste buds to the native delicacies at the local Warungs (small family-owned restaurants). These eateries offer traditional Indonesian and Balinese cuisine at an affordable price, allowing you to immerse yourself in the rich and flavoursome culinary culture of Kuta, Bali.

13.

Try the Amazing Upside Down World Bali

Upside Down World Bali includes seven uniquely designed rooms with décor and furniture purposefully set on the ceilings. Spend a pleasant day out with your significant other or friends and strike lots of bizarre stances that will appear in images as gravity-defying stunts. Families should visit Upside Down World in Bali. Selfie mode on cameras doesn't really work here, but the helpful staff will help you take pictures and lead you around the confusing chambers. This is one of the best activities to do in Kuta, you will enjoy it so much.

14.

Relish on Tranquil Vihara Dharmayana

One of the oldest Buddhist temples in Kuta is called Vihara Dharmayana. The temple's main buildings, which date back to 1876 and feature exquisite Far Eastern architecture and vibrant colours, are well-maintained. The altars' continual sputtering smoke from incense fills the quiet courtyard. Vihara Dharm Ayana Kuta is about a kilometre east of Kuta's main beach strip and is a little hidden on a side street. It is one of the coolest things to do in Kuta after a long day.

15.

Bali Underwater Scooter

An underwater garden off the shores of Nusa Ceningan, around a 30-minute boat ride southeast of Bali, is where Bali Underwater Scooter runs submersible scooters. These single-seater Walrus S-1 scooters can go up to 2 km/h because of their modest motors and long steering flaps. You will travel past coral gardens and unique marine animals with the assistance of trained divers. The breathing device has superb visibility throughout the tour and resembles a space helmet. You will have such a memorable experience and now add it to your list of best activities to do in Kuta.

16.

Go for a shopping haul at the Poppies Lane Kuta

For those who love shopping, a trip to Poppies Lane is a must-have experience during your visit to Kuta. This bustling shopping area is filled with small shops and boutiques that sell everything from clothes and accessories to souvenirs. It's a great place to find unique items, and one of the top Bali things to do in Kuta.

17.

Participate in Kuta Kite Festival

Kuta is known for its beautiful beaches, and participating in the annual Kuta Kite Festival is definitely one of the most enjoyable things to do in Kuta Beach. Held every year, this vibrant festival showcases a variety of colourful, massive kites soaring in the coastal sky. Joining in this jubilant celebration will surely create unforgettable memories while you're in Bali. Usually, the Kuta Beach Festival takes place every August, and it includes the Kuta Karnival, which features a kite festival.

18.

Fly Kites in Bali with your Family

A safe and best activity to do in Kuta with family is flying a kite. Are you wondering where you can get one? Trendy models, such as flying foxes and sailing ships with rainbow-coloured sails, are freely accessible from a line-up of small businesses and street vendors. Fly traditional Balinese kites like the Bebean (fish-shaped), Janggan (bird-shaped), and Pecukan (leaf-shaped) to emulate the natives. Choose a design, buy it, then head to a desolate area of Kuta Beach where it will be breezy and let it fly.

19.

Go on a Pleasant Visit to the Bali Bombing Memorial

Building the Bali Bombing Memorial, also known as Ground Zero, served as a way to pay eternal respect to those who perished in the Legian explosion in 2002. It replaces the demolished Paddy's Pub, which formerly stood on the main Legian road and is situated on the boundary between Kuta and Legian. Since then, the bar has moved to Jalan Legian and changed its name to Paddy's: Reloaded. The names and nationalities of the dead are inscribed on a massive marble plaque that is set into the beautifully carved sandstone of the Ground Zero memorial. The local government keeps the monument in good condition, and the best thing to do in Kuta at night is to visit this memorial all lit up. Every year on October 12th, a ceremony is held in memory of the victims.

20.

Caress the baby turtles at the Kuta Conservation Centre

Next to the Balawista lifeguard tower is a sea turtle hatchery and instructional centre called the Kuta Sea Turtle Conservation Site. Since 2011, the centre has collaborated with the Bali Sea Turtle Society to release more than 10,000 hatchlings. It prepares for the mating season, which lasts from June to July and sees the hatching of thousands of turtle eggs on temporary locations scattered around Kuta's beaches. The centre's striking sea turtle statue acts as a hatching area and a popular photo location in Kuta. For information on when you can participate in a "baby turtle race," check their website. Don't miss this activity on your list of best things to do in Kuta Indonesia.

21.

Go to Bali’s Finest Golf Court

New Kuta Golf is one of Bali's finest but most demanding 18-hole championship courses. Its lush, Indian Ocean-facing scenery was sculpted out of white limestone cliffs. Views of Balangan Beach and the Indian Ocean are available from Hole 15, which has been dubbed "The Cliff Hanger" as its hallmark hole. The golf club offers a pro shop, dining areas, conference rooms, locker rooms, and a large reception area for championship tournaments. International and Indonesian cuisine is offered at The Links Restaurant. The on-site spa center at New Kuta Golf offers Balinese massages and treatments. Golf lovers, this is the best thing to do in Kuta!

22.

Participate in Balinese Cooking Classes at Kuta

Add a Balinese cooking class to your things to do in the Kuta checklist. While visiting Bali, you can discover all different kinds of culinary schools, from traditional to gourmet. You can gain practical experience while learning to make a multi-course meal by enrolling in local cooking classes. You will learn all you need to know to prepare genuine Balinese food, from satay to curries. In addition to learning how to cook, your host will share anecdotes about what it's like to live in Bali as a local. For instance, our host explained to us the values of a typical Balinese family, as well as the cultural significance of the cuisine we were preparing. You will typically learn how to make 5-8 dishes during a half-day of cooking instruction.

23.

Explore the Famous Kuta Night Market

If you're seeking free activities to do in Kuta at night, you must go to a night market. Even though there are night markets all around Bali, the Kuta Night Market is one of the best. You may buy everything from clothing to regional treasures as you browse the market. Walking around the night market will be pure bliss for food lovers. You can eat as much as you want at affordable prices. The Open Night Market and Kuta Market are two other markets in the area. You may enjoy Kuta tourism while escaping the hectic downtown pace at these markets.

24.

Take a spiritual tour of the Masceti Temple

Among the various cultural and spiritual activities on offer, taking a tour of Masceti Temple stands out as one of the best things to do in Kuta Indonesia. Locally known as 'Pura Masceti,' this beautiful temple offers insight into Balinese Hinduism and its stunning architecture. During your visit, don't miss seeing traditional rituals and ceremonies when locals pay their respects to their ancestors.

25.

Taste and buy chocolates at the Heavenly Chocolate Bali

If you are a chocolate lover, visiting Heavenly Chocolate Bali should be on your list of things to do near Kuta. This boutique chocolate factory offers an array of delectable handmade chocolates using unique ingredients like mangosteen and chilli. Tour the factory, taste their delightful creations, and take home a sweet souvenir from your Bali trip.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) about Kuta, Bali
How can one engage in spiritual activities in Kuta?

Take a spiritual tour of Masceti Temple to learn about Balinese Hinduism, and for a sweet experience, visit Heavenly Chocolate Bali to taste and buy unique handmade chocolates.

What unique shopping experiences does Kuta offer?

Shop for souvenirs, clothes, and local handicrafts at Kuta Beachwalk, explore the bustling Poppies Lane for unique items, and visit the Kuta Night Market for an authentic shopping experience.

Are there family-friendly attractions in Kuta?

Yes, Kuta provides family-friendly fun such as the Circus Workshop for kids, the Circus Waterpark with exciting slides, and the Kuta Conservation Centre where you can interact with baby turtles.

What cultural experiences can one explore in Kuta?

Immerse yourself in Balinese culture by attending the Kuta Theater, joining a Balinese cooking class, and visiting Vihara Dharmayana, one of the oldest Buddhist temples in the area.

What are the best water activities to enjoy in Kuta, Bali?

Kuta offers an array of water adventures, including surfing at Kuta Beach, thrilling water slides at Waterbom Bali, and flowboarding at Armada Flow House.
