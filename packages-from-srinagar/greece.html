How to Reach Greece from Srinagar  

Getting to Greece from Srinagar is a breeze, thanks to well-connected flight routes. You'll start your journey at Srinagar International Airport, where you'll catch a flight to your chosen Greek destination. Athens, Thessaloniki, and Santorini are popular entry points for travelers from Srinagar. Our Greece tour packages ensure seamless flights, so you can relax and enjoy the anticipation of your adventure.

Greece Tour Package Highlights  

1. Ancient Marvels: Greece is filled with rich history and culture. Explore iconic landmarks like the Acropolis and Delphi. You can pay a visit to the Palace of Knossos, which is also a famous historical landmark. Witness history come alive as you wander through these ancient sites.

2. Stunning Islands: Greece boasts some of the world's most beautiful islands. Our Greece tour packages includes visits to Santorini and Mykonos, where you can soak up the sun on pristine beaches and marvel at the iconic blue-domed churches.

3. Mouthwatering Cuisine: Greek cuisine is a delight for food lovers. Savor authentic dishes like moussaka, souvlaki, and baklava at local tavernas. Don't forget to pair your meal with a glass of Ouzo or local wine.

4. Scenic Landscapes: Greece's natural beauty is unparalleled. Hike through the Samaria Gorge, relax on the beaches of Zakynthos, or witness the breathtaking sunsets of Oia in Santorini.

5. Vibrant Nightlife: Greece comes alive after dark. Experience the vibrant nightlife in Athens and Mykonos, where clubs and bars keep the party going until the early hours.
